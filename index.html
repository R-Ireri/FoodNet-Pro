About Us

Food Distribution System

In as much as there are quite a number of people who are willing to give a helping hand, there is no efficiency in the whole process. There are organizations that specialize in food and commodity distribution however, they are not able to reach a greater population.

The aim of this system is to create a proper and easy channel for people to donate food and other commodities to hunger and drought-stricken areas. This will ensure a consistent supply of food to the people living in such areas.
